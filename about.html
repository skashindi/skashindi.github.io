About Me

This page is about me. let me tell you a bit about myself.

I love computers and making things with them. I started learning about coding when I was in school and it quickly became something I was really excited about.

My dream is to use technology to make life easier and better. I'm always up for a challenge and love learning new things.

My Background

I've always been interested in technology, especially coding. Even when I was young, I liked figuring out how gadgets worked. This interest led me to learn more about computers and coding.

Through my education, I've learned many programming languages and gained a good understanding of computer science. This background helps me work on big and interesting projects in the tech world.

My Hobbies

I love soccer! I've been playing it since I was little. It's so much fun and keeps me active and healthy.

During weekends, I'm usually on the field, playing with my team. It's not just a game; it's about teamwork and working together.

When I'm not playing soccer or working with tech, I like to read, paint, and go for walks in nature. These things help me relax and get new ideas.

Whether I'm scoring goals on the fields or painting a picture, I'm happy and passionate about both soccer and tech.

My vision

I believe in working together and always learning new things, and I'm always ready for new challenges.
